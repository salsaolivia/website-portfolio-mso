About Me

Hello! I’m Meisya salsha Olivia, a passionate student from SMAN 12 Wajo, Keera District.

I believe that learning and growing come from embracing many paths — from the discipline of martial arts to the creativity of design, and the analytical world of science.

I have been actively practicing Pencak Silat, a traditional martial art that has taught me resilience, focus, and perseverance. Alongside that, I nurture my creative side through design and video editing, allowing me to express ideas and tell stories visually.

Academically, I have participated in the District-Level Science Olympiad (OSN IPA), which sharpened my scientific thinking, and completed journalism training to enhance my communication skills.

Through all these experiences, I strive to be someone who is strong in spirit, creative in mind, and passionate about continuous growth. Welcome to my journey!
